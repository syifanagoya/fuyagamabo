    Lindsay Lohan Husband: Meet Bader Shammas: Lindsay Lohan, officially known as Lindsay Dee Lohan is an American actress and singer born on July 2, 1986.

    She developed a love for acting and singing at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts.

    Lindsay kicked off her career as a child model with Ford Models at the age of three and modeled for Calvin Klein Kids and Abercrombie, among others.

    She also appeared in over 60 television commercials for brands like Pizza Hut and Wendy’s, as well as a Jell-O spot with Bill Cosby

    Having appeared as a regular on the television soap opera Another World at age 10, her breakthrough came in the Walt Disney Pictures film The Parent Trap.

    The film’s success led to appearances in several television films; Life Size and Get A Clue, as well as the big-screen productions Freaky Friday and Confessions Of A Teenage Drama Queen.

    Lohan’s early work won her childhood stardom, while the teen comedy sleeper hit Mean Girls affirmed her status as a teen idol and established her as a Hollywood leading actress.

    She also starred in the comedies; Herbie: Fully Loaded and Just My Luck. In order to showcase her range, Lohan began choosing roles in independent films such as; A Prairie Home Companion, Bobby, and Chapter 27.

    Lohan made her stage debut in the London West End production of Speed-the-Plow, starred in the second season of the comedy series Sick Note, and served as a panelist in the first season of Masked Singer Australia.

    As a singer, she signed with Casablanca Records and released two studio albums, Speak (platinum-certified) and A Little More Personal (gold-certified).

    Between 2016 and 2018, she opened three beach clubs in Greece, which were the focus of the MTV reality television series Lindsay Lohan’s Beach Club.

    After signing a multi-picture deal with Netflix, Lohan starred in the romantic comedy Falling For Christmas which was released last year (2022)

    In March 2023, Lindsay Lohan made headlines after she announced that she was pregnant and expecting her first baby with her husband, Bader Shammas.

    She made this announcement on Tuesday, March 14, 2023, via her Instagram account alongside a photo of a white bodysuit that reads “coming soon,” Lohan wrote, “We are blessed and excited!” tagging Shammas in the post.
