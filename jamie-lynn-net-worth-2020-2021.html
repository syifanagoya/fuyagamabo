    Early Life

    Career

    Jamie Lynn Spears debut movie was in 2002 alongside her sister Britney Spears in a comedy-drama titled “Crossroads”, the movie was commercially successful. Her sister, Britney Spears wrote “Follow Me” song and Jamie recorded it to be used as the series song. Jamie Lynn Spears had guest appearances in 2 series including “Just Jordan” and “Miss Guided”.
