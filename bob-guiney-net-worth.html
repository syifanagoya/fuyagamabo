    What is Bob Guiney's Net Worth?

    Bob Guiney is an American television personality who has a net worth of $1.5 million. Bob Guiney was born in Riverview, Michigan in May 1971. Guiney first appeared as a contestant on the reality television series The Bachelorette in 2003 for the first season. This led him to become the star of the reality TV series The Bachelor in the fourth season.
