Ahead of his first show of the autumn, Graham revealed the most outrageous request he's ever had from a female guest – nine dressing rooms for herself, and an extra one to charge her telephone.

      Graham, 56, spilled the beans earlier in September when he appeared on to promote the release of his book, r, in the US. When asked about the strangest request a guest has ever made, Graham explained: "You get used to it but the one that kind of took our breath away… This person will remain nameless. I'll call her 'she'. She wanted for herself nine dressing rooms and we go, 'fine here's your nine dressing rooms.'"

      Graham will be joined by Dame Helen Mirren, RuPaul and Jack Whitehall

      But even after catering to such an extravagant request, the presenter said that the guest in question was still not satisfied. The TV star added: "That afternoon, I'm sitting in the production office and one of her minions comes into us and says: "'It's a 911 situation. We need another dressing room.' So, Catherine, our line producer, is very organised, she gets on the phone: 'I'll get you another dressing room.'"
